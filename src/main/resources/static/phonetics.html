📘 Level 1 – Foundation Phonics (Ages 3–5)▼
Letter recognition (A–Z), single letter sounds, CVC blending (cat, dog, sun), rhyming & word families, first 100 sight words, simple sentence reading. Duration: 6 months
📗 Level 2 – Advanced Phonics (Ages 5–8)▼
Digraphs (sh, ch, th), vowel teams (ea, oa, ai), R-controlled vowels, silent letters, magic-e patterns, multi-syllable words, reading fluency with expression. Duration: 6 months
🎯 Course Benefits▼
Reading fluency, clear pronunciation, strong spelling, language confidence, improved academic performance across all subjects. Students typically reach 1–2 grade levels above peers.
🏫 Class Format▼
3 classes/week × 45 minutes. Max 8 students per batch. Available online (live video) and offline. Daily 10-minute home practice activities provided by the trainer.
