About the Course

Smart Write Program

Structured 3-level program to develop neat, fast, confident handwriting for ages 4–12.

4–12
Age Group
40 wpm

3 Levels
Foundation → Mastery
📘 Level 1 – Foundation (Ages 4–6)▼
Pencil holding, posture, pre-writing strokes, uppercase formation A–Z, numerals 0–9, letter height on ruled lines. 3 months
📗 Level 2 – Development (Ages 6–9)▼
Lowercase a–z, word spacing, baseline consistency, introduction to cursive joining, building writing speed. 4 months
📕 Level 3 – Mastery (Ages 9–12)▼
Full cursive writing, 40+ words per minute, personal style development, exam writing techniques. 4 months
