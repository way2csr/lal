Why Calligraphy?

More Than Beautiful Writing

✋

Fine Motor Skills

Precise pen control develops fine motor coordination that improves overall handwriting, drawing, and even sports performance.

🧘

Mindfulness & Focus

The deliberate, rhythmic nature of calligraphy is meditative — reducing stress and building the ability to focus deeply.

🎨

Artistic Expression

Children discover their unique artistic voice through lettering, creating personalized artwork, cards, and projects.

⭐

Academic Advantage

Beautiful, legible handwriting impresses teachers and earns higher marks in all written examinations and assignments.

Our Syllabus

3-Level Mastery Program

1
Beginner – Foundation
Ages 5–8 • 4 Months
▸Pen holding & posture fundamentals
▸Basic strokes & warm-up exercises
▸Lowercase italic alphabets (a–z)
▸Consistent letter height & spacing
▸Simple words & short sentences
2
Intermediate – Refinement
Ages 8–12 • 4 Months
▸Uppercase alphabets & numerals
▸Roman capital letterforms
▸Paragraph writing with uniform style
▸Introduction to Copperplate basics
▸Decorative borders & flourishes
3
Advanced – Mastery
Ages 12–15 • 4 Months
▸Copperplate & pointed pen technique
▸Multi-script artistic compositions
▸Certificate & invitation design
▸Color calligraphy with watercolor inks
▸Personal portfolio creation
