Beginner's Guide

Layer-by-Layer Method (7 Steps)

The universal beginner's method to solve any 3×3 Rubik's Cube — start here!

Step 1
🤍 White Cross
Form a white cross on top face with edges matching centre colours on each side face.
No algorithm needed — intuitive
Step 2
🤍 White Corners
Place white corner pieces to complete the white face. Use the right-hand algorithm.
R U R' U' (repeat)
Step 3
🔷 Middle Layer
Insert the four middle layer edges by moving them left or right.
U R U' R' U' F' U F
Step 4
💛 Yellow Cross
Create a yellow cross on the top face (ignore corner positions for now).
F R U R' U' F'
Step 5
💛 Orient Yellow Edges
Move the yellow edge pieces so they match their side-face centre colours.
R U R' U R U2 R'
Step 6
💛 Position Yellow Corners
Cycle the yellow corners until each corner is in its correct position.
U R U' L' U R' U' L
Step 7
✅ Orient Yellow Corners
Twist the yellow corners in place to complete the cube!
R' D' R D (repeat)
🏆 Done!
